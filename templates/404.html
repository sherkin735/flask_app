'I refuse to prove that I exist,' says Page, 'for proof denies faith, and without faith I am nothing.' 'But,' says Man, 'The Babel fish is a dead giveaway, is it not? It could not have evolved by chance. It proves you exist, and so therefore, by your own arguments, you don't. QED.' 'Oh dear,' says Page, 'I hadn't thought of that,' and promptly disappears in a puff of logic.
